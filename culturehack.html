A cultural project bridging the gap between tradition and modernity, introducing young Arab diaspora individuals in London to the valuable tradition of Arabic calligraphy through workshops and digital experiences.

Project Repository: GitHub

Key Features

  • In-person Workshops: Three main workshops covering history of calligraphy, traditional Arabic writing, and Calligraphy+Graffiti with "Calligraphiti" concepts
  • Digital Expansion: Educational masterclasses and online seminars for worldwide accessibility
  • Community Building: Platform for connecting individuals with similar cultural interests
  • Calligraphy Kits: Personalized tools including qalam and ink for workshops and online purchase

Technical Implementation

Developed a full-stack system for the CMCI department in under 5 hours featuring:

  • PostgreSQL database hosted on AWS
  • Backend powered by Python, Flask, and PyTorch
  • Clean UI styled with CSS
  • Integration of NeuralFusionII for real-time course summaries
Large Language Models Python PyTorch HTML Feature Engineering Attention Mechanisms

Benefits & Social Impact

  • Cultural Heritage Preservation: Helping young Arabs reconnect with their heritage creatively
  • Identity Strengthening: Providing Arabs living abroad with a stronger sense of cultural connection
  • Community Creation: Promoting communication across generations within the diaspora
  • Creative Innovation: Demonstrating how traditional cultural heritage can adapt in the digital age
